Group Project — Level Design

I was assigned to work with and lead a team for almost three months in order to develop a 3D dungeon crawler called Lost Souls. My role for this project was level design. I designed some rooms and was in charge of the overall lighting for the project.

Boss Room Design

The first room I created was the Boss room, where the main action was supposed to take place. Using Unity Assets, I designed this room using props and walls in order to create a dark dungeon feel.

Second Room

I thought that shape diversity would be a good idea to keep the players interested. Furthermore, the corners of this room turned out to be very useful in creating shadows, that would help in hiding enemies.

Boss Room — Lighting

Adding lights was a real adventure. Finding the right light temperature, colour, type, and direction was a long and difficult process until I figured it out. For this project, I thought that dimmed red candle-like lights would fit our theme the best. The action takes place in a post-apocalyptic ancient world, this requires a dark, cold atmosphere which I believe was perfectly created with the lights settings used.

Boss Room — Lighting

I chose to add different types of light in the same scene, to experiment with the colours and the shadows. The back of the room features a wall that goes further back into the room, making placing the same lights used for the rest of the room redundant and not visible. Therefore, I designed a candle holder that would have more small lights all around it. This choice proved to be a good one since the scene looks more lively.
