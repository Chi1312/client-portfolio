About me

About my passion

I can still remember the time I bought my first game for my first PC. It was Assassin’s Creed I, and it absolutely hooked me. I was so quickly immersed in the world and the story, that time and the real world didn’t exist anymore. The music, the combat, climbing all those buildings and taking leaps of faith from high peaks gave me a freedom I have never thought I could feel. This was the moment that made me fall in love with games and made me curious about the process. How can someone create a whole other dimension where I can experience all these incredible events? I wanted to do that. I wanted to be able to design and create magic experiences that would make everyone fall in love with games just the way I did. And so, I made everything I could to go on this wonderful journey.
